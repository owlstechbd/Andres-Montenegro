About DEL Program

The Doctor of Executive Leadership program at the University of Charleston is designed to equip leaders with the advanced skills necessary to navigate organizational challenges and drive sustainable change. Through coursework, research, and practical applications, the program emphasizes strategic leadership, ethical decision-making, and transformative leadership practices that inspire innovation and foster resilience in diverse organizational settings.

The Imperative of Resilient Leadership

Leadership is not defined in moments of ease, but in the complexity of adversity. It is in the everyday challenges—both personal and professional—where true leadership reveals itself. The essence of effective leadership lies in resilience: the ability to endure, adapt, and respond with clarity and courage when pressure intensifies. Resilient leadership equips individuals not only to lead others but to navigate their own internal struggles with purpose and integrity. In high-stakes fields such as security, organizational development, and personal legacy-building, resilience serves as both a defensive posture and a forward-moving strategy. It creates the foundation for sustainable leadership—where decisions are not made from panic or ego, but from reflection, preparedness, and long-term vision.

The development of resilience, however, does not occur in isolation. It requires experiences that test one’s beliefs, sharpen one’s responses, and demand accountability at every level. This professional and academic journey has emphasized that leadership development must be anchored in both intellectual rigor and lived reality. In that reality, feedback plays a crucial role. The ability to give, receive, and act upon direct feedback is a leadership muscle that, when combined with resilience, produces transformation. This is not just theoretical—it is the core of my doctoral focus: the development of resilience and leadership through direct feedback. Each artifact in this document is a reflection of that intersection, revealing how leadership is strengthened by adversity and sharpened through honest reflection and courageous action.
